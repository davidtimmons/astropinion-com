A purchased research population can flesh out discovery interviews and answer surveys to give you quick results. However, these users are not authentic. That leaves teams wondering if real product users would have given the same responses.

Real users can also be more motivated to give useful feedback. After all, they are paying you money or time to use your product and want a stellar experience.

To get authentic user feedback fast so you can build brilliant products.

If your team already conducts research with your actual product users, you know the process can be very time consuming.

For starters, you must first find users who are willing to take part in your research. Then, you need to record enough information about each user to adequately screen them for your research needs. After all that work, you must also carefully manage the number of touches each user receives so as not to cause research fatigue.

While managing a research population is a necessary job for every product team, it takes time away from the actual discovery and synthesis work product teams would rather do.

Astropinion is your mission control center for stellar user research. Use it to speed up the research process so you can focus on building brilliant products.
