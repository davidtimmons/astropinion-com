Product teams that want authentic user insights. In other words, hardworking product managers, researchers, analysts, designers, and marketers who want to launch stellar user research.

We all know it can be a struggle to get users to participate in interviews, surveys, and other discovery tasks.

When you maintain your own active research population, recruiting users to validate concepts or test ideas becomes far easier and more authentic. Managing this group of research participants is panel management.

While you can buy superficial research participants to pad discovery interviews and answer surveys, those users are not your real users. That leaves teams wondering if real users would have given the same responses.

Real users are also more motivated to give useful feedback. After all, our users are our partners in building brilliant products.

Astropinion is your mission control center for stellar user research. Use it to gain authentic responses to your questions with genuine insight into what your real users actually think.
